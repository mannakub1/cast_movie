<section id="audience_reviews" class="panel panel-rt panel-box ">
    <h2 class="panel-heading">Audience Reviews for <em>Guardians of the Galaxy</em>
</h2>
    <div class="panel-body content_body">
        <div>
                    <div class="quote_bubble fl cl">
    <div id="quoteBubble1816" class="review_quote">
        <div class="media">
            <div class="media-body">
                <div class="starHeader">
                    <span class="glyphicon glyphicon-star"></span><span class="glyphicon glyphicon-star"></span><span class="glyphicon glyphicon-star"></span><span class="glyphicon glyphicon-star"></span>
</div>
                <p class="comment clamp clamp-6">
                   Guardians was a victim of hype for me. I wanted to see it in theaters because everyone was telling me how amazing it was, but I just never got to it. After watching it, I can say it is overrated but enjoyable. Most people told me it was the best Marvel film to date and I highly disagree. While the special effects and action are typically top notch, the story leaves alot to be desired. Characters motivations change in an instant and it is completely unbelievable. Obviously the characters themselves all have unique and interesting personalities and there's a couple memorable ones, but I found the film lacking the punch necessary to take it from good to great. I still prefer The Avengers or the first Iron Man as the top of the Marvel canon so far.</p>
