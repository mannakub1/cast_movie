                   The story of a woman walking the Pacific Crest Trail in the mid-90s is interrupted by flashbacks, in which we slowly learn what made her go there in the first place. But Witherspoon is excellent in both time frames and holds them together. The cinematography is great and the ending very satisfying and touching.
